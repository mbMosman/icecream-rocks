Ice-Cream Rocks!

My favorite thing is ice-cream. I think it's awesome and I could eat it forever.

Favorite Ice-Cream Spots

These are a few of my favorite spots to get ice-cream in the Twin cities.

  • Izzy's
  • Pumphouse Creamery
  • Grand Ole Creamery
  • Milkjam Creamery

Favorite Treats

One of my favorite ice-cream treats is to take plain and simple chocolate ice-cream and top it with one of the flavored Kahlua liquors. Hazelnut Kahlua is my favorite, but the Salted Caramel is also pretty good.
